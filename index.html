ABOUT US

colors of rainbow is an biannual fest organized independently. It aims on developing the inter and intra communication and creative skills school kids of different regions. the purpose of conducting this fest in a grass root level is to nurture knowledge and creative ideas among the younger generation.

forthcoming it's first edition, it's exclusive for the students of primary grades. the idea being they are at their growing age.
